The Parable of the Net

The kingdom of heaven is like a net that was thron into the sea and gathered fish of every kind.

When it was full,men drew it ashore and sat down and sorted the good into containers but threw away the bad.

So it will be at the close of the age. The angels will come out and separate the evil from the righteous and throw them into the fiery furnace. in that place there will be weeping ang gnashing of teeth.
